Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my business is already listed?

Search for your business on our provider map. If you see your company, you can claim it using the form above. If not, submit a new listing request.

Is listing my business really free?

Yes! Basic listings are completely free. We may offer optional premium features in the future (priority placement, featured listings), but your basic listing will always be free.

How do I get leads from DumpsterMap?

When customers view your listing and request a quote, we'll send their contact information to you via email. You can then follow up directly with the customer.

Can I update my listing information?

Once you claim your listing, you'll be able to update your business information, add photos, and respond to any questions from potential customers.

Where does the review data come from?

We display your Google Business Profile reviews to help customers make informed decisions. Keep your Google reviews strong for the best results!
