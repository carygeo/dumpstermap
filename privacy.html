Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

DumpsterMap ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number when you request quotes or contact us
  • Location data: ZIP code or address to find providers in your area
  • Usage data: How you interact with our site, including pages visited and searches performed

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Connect you with dumpster rental providers in your area
  • Process your quote requests and share them with relevant providers
  • Improve our website and services
  • Communicate with you about your requests
  • Send promotional communications (with your consent)

Information Sharing

When you request a quote, we share your contact information and project details with the dumpster rental providers you select or who serve your area. This allows them to contact you with pricing and availability.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how you use our site
  • Improve our services

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may not work properly.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to the information we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications

Contact Us

For questions about this privacy policy or our practices, contact us at:

support@dumpstermap.io

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We'll notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
